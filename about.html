About Me

Discover my interests, education journey, and future aspirations

My Journey

I'm a 3rd-year student at Kherson State University, majoring in Information Systems and Technologies. My educational journey began at Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute, where I studied Applied Mathematics, which significantly developed my statistical thinking and analytical approach.

Currently, I'm balancing my studies with work as a Sales Manager, where I apply my analytical skills to improve business processes and enhance customer interactions.

Interests & Passions

Beyond technical aspects, I have a deep passion for design and fashion. I enjoy analyzing trends, experimenting with styles, and creating aesthetically pleasing solutions. This helps me develop creative thinking that I can apply to my technical work, particularly in UI/UX design.

I'm also dedicated to learning foreign languages - English, Japanese, and French. Languages represent an expansion of horizons and an opportunity to better understand different cultures, people, and ways of thinking.

Mathematical logic and cryptography represent another area of my interests. I enjoy solving complex problems and analyzing algorithms, which makes working in IT even more fascinating.

Future Aspirations

In the future, I plan to continue developing in the field of information technology, focusing on business analytics and project management. I'm interested in combining technical knowledge with business process understanding to create effective solutions.

I also intend to continue studying foreign languages and potentially gain experience working in an international company where I can apply all my skills and knowledge.
